How to book an appointment:

  1. Choose the type of cut you want. Whether it's a clean shave, beard trim, or haircut, we have you covered!

    We offer discounts on combinations of beard and hair grooming, as well as a lower price for all children under 10.


  2. Pick a date that you'd be able to pass by.

  3. Choose a time slot that's most convenient for you.

    *Please note that we're not open before noon(12pm) on Sundays.


  4. Enter your first and last names, as well as phone number and email address.

  5. Once you've filled out all your info, add any special requests you want and click Submit Appointment!
