About Us

Everything started a couple of years ago for Jorge. He really wanted to start his own business. He knew he had the talent and the persistance that someone needs to walk the road of becoming their "own boss".

After spending countless nights planning a new start, he decided to open his own Barbershop in the Ville-Emard area, a quiet neighbourhood in the South-west of Montreal. His next step was to get the word out by promoting his new place and attracting as many new costumers as he could!

One of the main goals for George was to make Mr. Corte a place where guys from the neighbourhood could go talk, laugh, share stories, and have as much fun as possible while getting the latest haircut trends available at a fair price.

Of Course, Mr. Corte became a great place to hang out. With the addition of new Barbers working with him and a modern-looking shop, he became the new center of attention on Allard Street. He has great music all the time, air-conditioning, a flatscreen tv with a video-game setup and a snack-bar always stocked! But one of the main attractions is "Mr. Chato", the official mascot of Mr. Corte Barbershop.
